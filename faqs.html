Frequently Asked Questions

General FAQs

Q: What is Smart Services?

Smart Services private limited is an integrated sport infrastructure company.

Q: What does Smart Services do?

Smart Services is well positioned to work with interested clients in providing world class sporting facilities, sporting clubs and recreational centers. Where youth and any age group can maximize their talents for productive use. Not only do we help to put these structures in place we also ensure these ventures facilities are active all the year round by developing and organizing various events.

Q: How to get in touch?

Our office is at Khaliq Complex, Solina Bazaar near petrol pump, Srinagar-190008, J&K, feel free to contact on +919797999431 or +919419410215

Q: What types of sports courts are included in this franchise?

Our franchise offers a variety of sports courts including Football 7aside, 5aside, basketball, tennis, pickleball, volleyball, and multi-sport courts designed for both indoor and outdoor use as per the land availability.

Q: What are the primary benefits of becoming a franchisee with your company?

Benefits include a proven business model, comprehensive training programs, ongoing support, marketing assistance, and access to high-quality equipment and materials.

Financial FAQS

Q: What is the initial investment required to open a sports court franchise?

The initial investment typically ranges from Rs. 18.00 Lacs to Rs. 20.00 Lacs depending on the location, size, and type of sports courts being developed.

Q: Are there any ongoing fees?

Yes, franchisees are required to pay ongoing royalties, which are typically a percentage of their monthly revenue, and a marketing fee to support national and local marketing efforts.

Q: What financing options are available?

No, We do not offer assistance in securing finance.

Operational FAQS

Q: What kind of training and support can I expect?

Franchisees receive comprehensive initial training covering all aspects of the business, including site selection, court construction, operations, marketing, and customer service. Ongoing support includes regular updates, additional training sessions, and access to our franchisee support team.

Q: How long does it take to open a franchise location?

The timeline from signing the franchise agreement to opening a location varies but generally takes between 1 to 2 months, depending on site selection, permitting, and construction including Finance.

Q: What is the process for selecting a location for my sports court franchise?

We provide assistance with site selection, including market analysis, demographic studies, and site visits to ensure optimal location and potential for success.

Marketing and Sales FAQS

Q: How will I attract customers to my sports courts?

We provide marketing support that includes national advertising campaigns, local marketing strategies, social media promotion, and access to branded materials. Additionally, we offer guidance on hosting events and partnerships with local schools and sports organizations. Basically, there are limited options on pay and play and we will assist you there to explore more options.

Q: Will I have a protected territory?

Yes, each franchisee is granted a protected territory to ensure there is no direct competition with other franchise locations within a specified area.

Construction and Maintenance FAQS

Q: What are the specifications for building a sports court?

We provide detailed specifications and guidelines for constructing sports courts, including recommended materials, dimensions, and required features. We also offer support during the construction process to ensure quality and compliance with our standards.

Q: Who is responsible for the maintenance of the sports courts?

Franchisees are responsible for the regular maintenance of their sports courts. We provide training and resources to help franchisees maintain the courts to ensure safety and quality. But, the Maintenance cost is chargeable.

Legal and Compliance FAQS

Q: What legal documents are required to become a franchisee?

Franchisees must review and sign the Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD) and the Franchise Agreement with Minimal Fee.

Q: Are there any regulatory requirements specific to sports courts?

No, there is no such requirement.

Miscellaneous FAQS

Q: Can I own multiple franchise locations?

Yes, we encourage successful franchisees to expand and own multiple locations. Multi-unit ownership opportunities are available for qualified franchisees.

Q: What kind of insurance do I need?

Franchisees are not as such required to carry any insurance/ property insurance, and worker’s compensation insurance. However, if so we provide guidance on the necessary coverage and can recommend preferred insurance providers.
