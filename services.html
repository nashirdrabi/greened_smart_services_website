What we do

Football

Synthetic Turf
The world-class synthetic turf has been finely crafted and engineered specifically to meet or exceed the FIFA Quality Pro and FIFA Quality standards. Our products have been used for international /National tournaments such as FIFA Women’s World Cup and Men’s international matches, Champions League matches, Super Bowl, to name a few.

Hybrid Turf
Hybrid grass systems combines the playing qualities of a natural grass surface with the durability of artificial turf. The water-retaining fiber technology gives 100% true-to-nature playing experience. It helps in optimal shock absorption and is uniform over the whole pitch. Regardless of the weather, the Tarkett hybrid turf system provides an all-weather playability condition all year round.

Hockey

Synthetic Turf
Our hockey fields develop fast pitches, true ball roll, increased player safety, and comfort resulting in skillful games, thus making it the most preferred choice. The surface offers player-friendly advancements to the game, enabling players to showcase their skills and strengths. Hockey players enjoy the comfortable cushioning and do not worry about severe injuries from gliding on our fields.
