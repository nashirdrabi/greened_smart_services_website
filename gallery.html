Personal Data: When you visit our website, we may ask for personal information, such as your name, email address, contact number, and address, which you voluntarily provide when filling out forms or subscribing to newsletters. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties. Greened Smart Services is committed to protecting your information. We use commercially acceptable methods to ensure your data’s security, although no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
